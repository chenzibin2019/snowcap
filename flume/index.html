Flume

A blazingly fast multi-producer, multi-consumer channel.

"Do not communicate by sharing memory; instead, share memory by communicating."

Example

let (tx, rx) = flume::unbounded();

tx.send(42).unwrap();
assert_eq!(rx.recv().unwrap(), 42);
